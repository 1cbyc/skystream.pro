# Attribution & NASA Usage

SkyStream.pro is powered by public data from the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). We are grateful for their commitment to making this information available to the public.

## Usage Guidelines

As a user or contributor to this project, please adhere to the following guidelines when displaying any content sourced from NASA:

1.  **Visible Attribution:** Always provide a clear and visible attribution to NASA on any page or component that displays their data or imagery. A simple "Image/data courtesy of NASA" or similar text is sufficient.

2.  **Respect Terms of Use:** All usage of the NASA APIs and content must comply with the official [NASA API Terms of Use](https://api.nasa.gov/).

3.  **No Endorsement:** The use of NASA's name, logo, or imagery must not imply endorsement by NASA of the SkyStream.pro project or any commercial products. This project is an independent effort and is not affiliated with or endorsed by NASA.

4.  **Observe Source Metadata:** Some NASA content may have specific rights or restrictions. It is important to observe any original source metadata and licensing information associated with the content.

## Example Attribution Text

Here is an example of a suitable attribution to be placed in the footer of the application:

> "Data & media provided by NASA. This project is not affiliated with or endorsed by NASA."
